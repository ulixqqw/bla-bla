Meteora

rocks consisting of a mixture of sandstone and clastic rock and reaching a
height of 600 m above sea level, in the mountains of Thessaly in northern Greece, in the north of the peripheral unit of Trikala of the periphery of Thessaly, 1-2 km north of the city of Kalambaka.
In 1988, the monasteries located on the tops of the cliffs were included in the list of World Heritage Sites.
The rocks were formed more than 60 million years ago and were the rocky bottom of a
prehistoric sea that was located on the site of a plain.
In total, throughout history, according to various sources,
there were 22 or 24 monasteries, along with many single cells, ascitiriums, chapels, hermitages,
caves, gates, pillars, scattered throughout the Meteor mountains.

but for many years after that it remained almost unknown to the general public.
The first European settlements appeared here after the declaration of US independence, when roads began to be built,
and the concept of “frontier” acquired a new meaning. As a result of the Anglo-American War of 1812-1815,
part of the waterfall ended up on the side of the United States, and the other part on the side of the British loyalists,

Lego Black Panther

Based on the film Civil War, the Black Panther wears a black suit with triangular vibranium
inserts and a claw necklace around his neck. The mask has shiny
ilver outlines and a pair of yellow
lenses; the contours continue on a separate piece with short ears. The updated suit from the solo film
reduced the presence of metal in favor of muscle-like contours, and replaced the lenses with holes to reveal dark skin
and eyes. The Kinetic Energy Suit added blue (later changed to purple) coloring throughout.
